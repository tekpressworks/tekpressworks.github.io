About

TEK Pressworks with the experience of more than 60 years has been a leader in manufacturing sheet metal pressed parts for various industries. We are renowned for manufacturing sheet metal deep drawn components, using a range of methods like cold forming and stamping.

With over 60 years of experience in the industry, we have established a name for effective time control and cost-saving strategies. We focus our efforts towards the productivity standard in our progress towards creating a perfect product.

Our skilled personnel is our strength. From operating machines to conducting thorough quality management at every step, our team ensures you receive the best products on every avenue. We orchestrate periodic in-house training to ensure our workers perform and deliver the best quality product.
